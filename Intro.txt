Web development: It include three main components that are as follows

1.HTML
2.CSS
3.JAVASCRIPT


HTML: 
    * It defines the overall physical structure of the Webpage(BODY).
    * It stands for Hypertext Markup Language
CSS: 
    * It is used to add effects on the webpage or to beautify the webpage content and its structure(BEAUTY).
    * It stands for cascading style sheets

Javascript: 
    * It is used to add logics to the website that is when a square or forward button is pressed what will happen and etc(BRAIN).
    * It helps in making Dynamic websites
